Upcoming Events

Durgotsav, 2023

Durgotsav, or Durga Puja, in 2023 marks a vibrant celebration of Goddess Durga's triumph over evil. It's a multi-day Hindu festival filled with colorful pandals, cultural events, prayers, and joyous gatherings. People adorn traditional attire, visit beautifully crafted idols of Goddess Durga, and immerse in the festive spirit of music, dance, and community feasts, symbolizing the victory of good over evil.

Kali Puja, 2023

Kali Puja in 2023 will be a revered Hindu festival honoring Goddess Kali's power and strength. Through prayers, rituals, and offerings, devotees seek her blessings for protection and prosperity. The festival signifies the victory of good over evil and fosters spiritual devotion amidst vibrant cultural celebrations.

Deepawali, 2023

Diwali, or Deepawali, in 2023 signifies light triumphing over darkness. Celebrated with colorful lights, sweets, and prayers, it's a joyous Hindu festival fostering togetherness, prosperity, and the victory of good over evil.
